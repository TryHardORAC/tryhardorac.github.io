Self-Improving Neural Pruning: A Graph Neural Network Framework for Scalable Mixed Bundle Pricing

Liangyu Ding, Chenghan Wu, Guokai Li, Zizhuo Wang

Under review

PDF arXiv

We study scalable mixed bundle pricing, where the seller must decide which product bundles to offer and how to price them under customer self-selection. The approach learns compact customer-product structure with a graph neural network, uses the predictions to prune the exponentially large bundle space, and then solves a smaller pricing optimization problem. A self-improvement loop further expands training to larger instances by using strong neural-pruning solutions as new labels.
